Guakia Nitayno

Humanitas Primoris / The Good People First

Note: This page honors the Taíno language of Borikén. Core concepts are presented in reconstructed Taíno-Arawak; philosophical expansions remain in English.

Core Tenets

  • Semign Ie. Spirit Is. (Being Precedes Judgment). Existence is sacred before it is useful.
  • Guakia Heketi. We Are One. (We are a Web). We are not separate from the tribe or the island.
  • Opia. Mortality (The condition of meaning). We accept the cycle of life and ancestorhood.
  • Guatiao Atabey. Amor Mundi: Friendship/Love of Mother Earth. We protect the waters and the mountains.
  • Guengue. Wisdom (Knowledge shared). Wisdom is for the community, not the self.

Articles of Rejection

We reject the following conditions:

  • Makata. The Calculus of Dehumanization (Unimportance). No person is "makata" (unimportant).
  • Ana-Karaya. Politics of Division (The Bad Moon). We reject hatred and separation.
  • Naboria Vaga. Tyranny of Labor (Forced work without honor). We are not beasts of burden.
  • Cemí Maquina. Idolatry of the Algorithm (Worship of the Machine). We do not worship tools.
  • Jibá. Politics of Deception (The Forest/Bush). We reject being lost in lies and hidden agendas.
  • Manicato. Gnostic Escape. We stay bold and brave here. We do not flee.

Conclusion

Daka Boricua. Daka Nitayno.

I am of this land. I am human.

Ajani (Return)
