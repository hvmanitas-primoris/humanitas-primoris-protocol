Lënapeowagan

Humanitas Primoris / Humanity First

Note: This translation honors the Lenape people. Core concepts are in Unami dialect; expansions are in English.

Core Tenets

  • Lënape Hoke. Being Precedes Judgment (The person/body is first). Existence is valid before profit.
  • Mawsihakèy. We are a Web. We are tied together in a circle, not standing alone.
  • Wewiton. Wisdom is the Goal. To understand is better than to just know.
  • Ahoalat Ëhki. Amor Mundi: Love the Earth/Land. We respect the soil we stand on.
  • Angel. Mortality. Death is a part of the circle, giving life its shape.

Articles of Rejection

We reject the following conditions:

  • Mëtaxsit. The Calculus of Dehumanization (Treating people as bad/worthless/evil).
  • Pëkèwit. Politics of Division (Breaking apart/Separation).
  • Mikemosawakon Mëtaxsit. Tyranny of Dehumanizing Labor. Bad work that hurts the person.
  • Mëchin-Sakima. Idolatry of the Algorithm (The Machine Chief).
  • Kanakwsu. Politics of Deception (He is deceitful).
  • Katschi Undach. Gnostic Escape (Gone from here / Leaving this place). We stay here.

Conclusion

Wanishi. Yu Tali.

Thank you. We are here.

Këpe (Return)
