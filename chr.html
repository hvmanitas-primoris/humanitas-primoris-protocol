Humanitas Primoris

Note: This is a foundational translation. Core tenets are presented in Tsalagi (Cherokee); philosophical expansions remain in English to ensure accuracy.

ᎢᏗᎦᎪᏘ (Core Tenets)

  • ᎡᎭ ᎢᎬᏱ ᎨᏒᎢ. Being Precedes Judgment; Existence is Inherent. We affirm that human dignity is not earned through metrics, labor, or algorithm, but is inherent to existence itself.
  • ᏴᏫ ᏌᏊ ᎢᎩ. We are a Web, Not a Collection of Atoms. Individualism is a myth. We are bound to one another and to the biosphere.
  • ᎠᏲᎱᎯᏍᏗ ᎨᏒ ᎪᎵᏍᏗ. Mortality Is the Condition of Meaning. We reject the techno-utopian desire to live forever. Limits give life value.
  • ᎡᎶᎯ ᎠᏓᎨᏳᏗ. Amor Mundi: A Radical Love of This World. We are not trying to escape to Mars or the Metaverse. We are stewards of *this* Earth.
  • ᎠᎦᏙᎲᏍᏗ ᏗᎨᎦᏗ. Wisdom Is the Goal. Information is cheap; wisdom is the synthesis of experience and morality.

ᏗᏲᎯᏍᏗ (Articles of Rejection)

We reject the following conditions:

  • ᎠᏎᏉ ᏗᏎᏍᏗ ᎬᏅᎢ. The Calculus of Dehumanization. We reject any equation that treats human life as a disposable variable.
  • ᎢᎸᏍᎩ ᎢᏳᏓᎴᎩ ᏓᎾᏓᎴᏂᏍᎬᎢ. Politics of Division and Purity. Racism, xenophobia, and tribalism are distractions from our common humanity.
  • ᎠᏎᏉ ᏗᎦᎸᏫᏍᏓᏁᏗ. Tyranny of Dehumanizing Labor. We oppose "bullshit jobs" and the reduction of humans to gears in a machine.
  • ᎧᏃᎮᎸᏗ ᎤᏂᎬᏫᏳᏌᏕᎩ. Idolatry of the Algorithm. Math is a tool, not a god. We reject algorithmic authoritarianism.
  • ᎤᏠᎾᏍᏗ ᏗᎧᎿᏩᏛᏍᏗ. Politics of Deception. We oppose propaganda, astroturfing, and the weaponization of truth.
  • ᎣᏍᏛ ᎪᎯᏳᏗ ᎬᏗ ᏓᎾᏟᎲᎢ. Weaponization of Faith. We reject those who use the divine to justify oppression or hatred.
  • ᎦᎸᎳᏗ ᏗᏓᏂᎸᎢᏍᏗ ᎤᎾᏚᎸᎲᎢ. Gnostic Escape. We reject the fantasy of abandoning our bodies or our planet for a digital heaven.

ᏙᎢ (Conclusion)

ᎠᏂ ᎡᎶᎯ ᎢᏕᎭ. ᎠᏂ ᎡᎶᎯ ᎢᏗᏍᏕᎵᏍᎩ. ᏴᏫ ᎢᎬᏱ.

We are here. We are stewards of this world. Humanity First.
