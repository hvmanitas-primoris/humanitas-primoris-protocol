Anishinaabe Nitam

Humanitas Primoris / Humanity First

Note: This is a foundational translation using the Double Vowel system. Core tenets are in Anishinaabemowin; expansions remain in English.

The Core Tenets

  • Bimaadiziwin Nitam. Being Precedes Judgment. Life comes first. We exist before we are measured.
  • Gakina Indinawemaaganidog. We are a Web (Literally: "All my relations"). We are connected to every living thing, not isolated individuals.
  • Nibwaakaawin. Wisdom is the Goal. Not just knowledge, but the right use of knowledge.
  • Zaagi'idiwin Aki. Amor Mundi: Love of the Earth/Land. We cherish the physical world we inhabit.
  • Aakde'ewin. Bravery. To face mortality without fear is the condition of meaning.

Articles of Rejection

We reject the following:

  • Gego Bimaadiziwin Ga-gikendaagwad. The Calculus of Dehumanization. We reject treating life as a number.
  • Baké-iziwin. Politics of Division. We reject being split apart by hatred.
  • Anokiiwin ga-bwanaawit. Tyranny of Dehumanizing Labor. Work that weakens the spirit.
  • Mazinigan-Ogimaa. Idolatry of the Algorithm (The "Paper Boss"). We do not bow to machines.
  • Gwiinawi-doodowin. Politics of Deception. The confusion caused by lies.
  • Babaamaadiziwin. Gnostic Escape. Wandering away from reality; the fantasy of leaving Earth.

Conclusion

Omaa Akiing. Omaa Gidaa-yami.

We are here on Earth. We stay here.

Azhe-giiwe (Return)
